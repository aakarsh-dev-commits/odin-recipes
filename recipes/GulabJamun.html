GULAB JAMUN

Melt-in-Your-Mouth Gulab Jamun Recipe!!

Ingredients

For the Jamuns (Dough Balls)

  • 1 cup milk powder
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 2 tablespoons ghee (clarified butter)
  • 2 to 3 tablespoons milk (as needed to make the dough)
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • A pinch of cardamom powder (optional)

For the Sugar Syrup:

  • 1 1/2 cups sugar
  • 11 1/2 cups water
  • 3 to 4 green cardamom pods (crushed)
  • 1 teaspoon rose water or a few drops of rose essence
  • A few saffron strands (optional)

For Frying:

  • Ghee or oil (enough for deep frying)

Steps

  1. Syrup: Boil sugar + water. Add cardamom, rose water, saffron. Simmer for 5 to 7 min. Keep warm.
  2. Dough: Mix dry ingredients. Add ghee + milk to form soft dough. Rest 10 min.
  3. Shape: Make smooth, small balls.
  4. Fry: On low-medium heat until golden brown.
  5. Soak: Place fried balls into warm syrup. Soak 1 to 2 hrs.
