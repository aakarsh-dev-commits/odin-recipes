Spaghetti Bolognese

Most Delecious Spaghetti Bolognese you are ever going to have!!

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 small onion (chopped)
  • 2 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 250g minced beef (or plant-based alternative)
  • 400g canned tomatoes (or tomato puree)
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil or parsley (optional)
  • Grated Parmesan (optional)

Steps

Cook Spaghetti

  1. Boil in salted water until al dente. Drain and set aside.

Make the Sauce

  1. Heat oil in a pan.
  2. Sauté onions for 2 to 3 mins, then add garlic.
  3. Add minced beef and cook until browned.
  4. Stir in tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, salt & pepper.
  5. Simmer for 15 to 20 mins until thickened.

Combine & Serve

  1. Toss spaghetti with the sauce or spoon sauce on top.
  2. Garnish with cheese and herbs if desired.
