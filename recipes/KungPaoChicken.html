Kung Pao Chicken

A fiery, flavorful stir-fry that hits the perfect balance of sweet, spicy, and savory.

Ingredients

For the Chicken:

  • 250g boneless chicken breast/thigh (cubed)
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp cornstarch

For the Sauce:

  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp vinegar (rice or white)
  • 1 tbsp hoisin sauce (optional)
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp cornstarch
  • 2 tbsp water

Stir-fry:

  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 2 to 3 dried red chilies
  • 2 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 1/2 inch ginger (chopped)
  • 1/4 cup bell peppers (cubed)
  • 2 tbsp roasted peanuts or cashews
  • Spring onion (for garnish)

Steps

Marinate Chicken

  1. Mix chicken with soy sauce & cornstarch.
  2. Let sit for 10–15 minutes.

Mix Sauce

  1. Combine all sauce ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Stir until smooth.

Stir-Fry

  1. Heat oil in a wok or pan.
  2. Add chilies, garlic, and ginger — stir for 30 seconds.
  3. Add chicken — cook until golden.
  4. Toss in bell peppers, stir-fry for 1–2 minutes.
  5. Pour in the sauce — stir until thickened.
  6. Add peanuts and toss well.

Serve

  1. Garnish with spring onions.
  2. Serve hot with rice or noodles.
